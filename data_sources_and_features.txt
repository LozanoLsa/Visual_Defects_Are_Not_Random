PROJECT: Visual_Defects_Are_Not_Random
TITLE: Data Sources & Feature Engineering

--------------------------------------------------
OVERVIEW:
This dataset integrates process parameters, environmental conditions, and material handling variables from a real industrial painting process.

The objective is to identify and predict surface defects by understanding how process variability and handling conditions impact final quality.

--------------------------------------------------
DATA SOURCES AND VARIABLE ORIGIN:

1. THERMAL / PROCESS CONTROL SYSTEM
Variables:
- regrind_pct
- resin_temp_c
- cooling_time_s

Taken from:
Injection molding and material preparation process logs.

Reason:
Material composition and thermal conditions directly affect surface integrity. Variations in resin temperature and cooling time influence part stability and defect formation.

--------------------------------------------------
2. PAINTING PROCESS PARAMETERS
Variables:
- paint_viscosity_cp
- film_thickness_um

Taken from:
Paint preparation system and in-line quality measurements.

Reason:
Paint viscosity and film thickness are critical for achieving uniform coverage. Deviations can lead to visual defects such as orange peel, runs, or insufficient coating.

--------------------------------------------------
3. ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS (PAINT BOOTH)
Variables:
- booth_humidity_pct

Taken from:
Paint booth environmental monitoring system.

Reason:
Humidity affects paint adhesion, drying behavior, and surface finish quality.

--------------------------------------------------
4. PRE-PROCESS HANDLING / STORAGE
Variables:
- pre_paint_storage_hrs

Taken from:
Material staging and buffer storage records prior to painting.

Reason:
Extended storage time can introduce contamination, moisture absorption, or surface degradation.

--------------------------------------------------
5. MATERIAL HANDLING CONDITIONS
Variables:
- num_handlings
- container_type
- part_protection

Taken from:
Internal logistics and handling process records.

Reason:
Handling frequency and protection methods influence the risk of scratches, contamination, and surface damage before painting.

--------------------------------------------------
6. TARGET VARIABLE (QUALITY INSPECTION)
Variables:
- surface_defect

Taken from:
Final visual inspection records (OK / NOK classification).

Reason:
Represents whether the part exhibits a surface defect after the painting process.

--------------------------------------------------
VARIABLE-BY-VARIABLE SUMMARY:

regrind_pct
Taken from:
Material composition records
Why it was included:
Higher regrind content can affect material consistency and surface quality.

resin_temp_c
Taken from:
Injection process monitoring
Why it was included:
Temperature impacts material flow and surface finish.

cooling_time_s
Taken from:
Molding cycle data
Why it was included:
Improper cooling can lead to internal stresses and surface defects.

paint_viscosity_cp
Taken from:
Paint preparation system
Why it was included:
Controls coating behavior and surface uniformity.

film_thickness_um
Taken from:
In-line thickness measurements
Why it was included:
Too thin or too thick coatings can generate defects.

booth_humidity_pct
Taken from:
Environmental sensors
Why it was included:
Humidity affects drying and adhesion.

pre_paint_storage_hrs
Taken from:
Material staging records
Why it was included:
Long exposure before painting increases contamination risk.

num_handlings
Taken from:
Handling process tracking
Why it was included:
More handling increases probability of surface damage.

container_type
Taken from:
Logistics configuration records
Why it was included:
Different containers offer different levels of protection.

part_protection
Taken from:
Handling procedure definition
Why it was included:
Protected vs unprotected parts behave differently in defect risk.

surface_defect
Taken from:
Quality inspection results
Why it was included:
Defines the outcome variable for classification.

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE ENGINEERING LOGIC:

The dataset combines process parameters, environmental conditions, and handling factors to capture the full lifecycle of the part before final inspection.

Each variable was selected due to its direct or indirect influence on surface quality.

--------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS LOGIC:

Surface defects are not random.

They are the result of interactions between material conditions, process parameters, environment, and handling practices.

Understanding these relationships allows early intervention before defects occur.

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:

Predict and reduce surface defects by:
- Identifying critical process variables
- Improving quality at the source (Jidoka)
- Reducing scrap and rework
- Stabilizing the painting process

--------------------------------------------------
NOTE:

Data originates from an industrial painting process, integrating material preparation, process control, environmental monitoring, and handling records.
